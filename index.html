Education

I have always valued education, and loved learning about new things! I was on the high honor role throughout high school, and am now attending BYU. I plan to graduate with a Marketing Degree and am so excited for what my future holds as far as learning goes!

Travel Experience

I have had lots of travel experience and enjoy exploring new parts of the world! I have lived in multiple different places and am open to moving anywhere! I have spent time in Utah, Nevada, The Dominican Republic, Mexico, Switzerland, Germany, Italy and more! I am experienced in travel and familiar with many different cultures. Guitar Performance

I have a variety of experiences with guitar performance as well as singing while I play. I have taken my guitar on most of my travels and played all around the world. I love to share music and hear music from others. It is a hobby I hope to keep up for the rest of my life!

Mentoring/Teaching Experience

I am currently working as an "Infield Mentor" at the Missionary Training Center in Provo, Utah. I get to meet with companionships of missionaries over zoom who have recently started their mission. I have the opportunity to teach, listen, share, and learn with missionaries located all throughout the world! I love the experience I am gaining working with people and building my faith at the same time. I have also gained more leadership skills as well as organized preperation skills.

Violin Performance

I have been playing the violin since the age of 7, and have been involved in many performance groups throughout my life. I played with The Young Artist Chamber Players for over 4 years, and traveled with them to play in Europe on a couple of occasions. While developing this skill to play the violin I learned patience, persistance, accountability, hard work, and responsibility. I won 3 awards and competed in 6 federations where I later earned the highest level award. I hope to continue playing throughout my life, and keeping it up as a hobby, even if it is just to play in church!

My Mission

At the age of 19 I had the wonderful opportunity to serve a full time mission for The Church Of Jesus Christ Of Latter Day Saints for 18 months. I moved to Las Vegas Nevada where I preached the good word of God to as many people as we could get to listen! I learned vital skills, made lifelong friends, and most importantly gained a testimony for my Savior Jesus Christ. My mission taught me independance, dilligance, patience, resilliance, virtue, love, and good study habits. I know that I will be able to apply what I learned during that time to the rest of my life forever!
